I'm Annabel Aguiar, a current Features reporting summer intern at the Washington Post. I'm a reporter, writer and designer originally from Tampa, Florida.

My work also has appeared in the Seattle Times and USAToday, as well as in my campus newspaper the Indiana Daily Student. At the IDS, I worked in a number of positions including Editor-in-Chief, Creative Director, Managing Editor, Enterprise Editor, Arts Editor, Design Editor and features reporter. I worked through two elections, a tornado and a pandemic (not all at once, though). I'm passionate about film, the arts, design, cooking and television.
