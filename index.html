I'm Annabel Aguiar, a State Issues reporter for the Lansing State Journal in Lansing, Michigan. I'm a reporter, writer and designer originally from Tampa, Florida.

My work also has appeared in the Seattle Times and USAToday. I graduated from Indiana University in 2021, where I worked for the campus newspaper the Indiana Daily Student. At the IDS, I held a number of positions including Editor-in-Chief, Creative Director, Managing Editor, Enterprise Editor, Arts Editor, Design Editor and features reporter. I worked through two elections, a tornado and a pandemic (not all at once, though). I'm passionate about film, the arts, design, cooking and television.

I'm a member of the National Association of Hispanic Journalists and the Society for News Design, and won awards for both design and reporting work while in college. I'm also a former participant of the New York Times Student Journalism Institute. Here's my resumé:
